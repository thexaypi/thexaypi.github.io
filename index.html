Questions? Answered.

What is included in "hosting"?+

Your website will be hosted on a fast, reliable server with uptime monitoring. No third-party hosting costs — everything is managed by Aitte.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?+

Plans are billed every 6 months or yearly. There is no lock-in beyond the billing period. You choose what works for your business.

How does the face-to-face meeting work?+

After initial email contact, Rotterdam-based clients receive the business address and can schedule a session. This ensures every project detail is properly aligned before work begins.

What does "logo creation included" mean?+

A professional logo is designed and delivered as part of your plan — no extra cost. It will be tailored to your brand and business identity.

Can I upgrade from CORE to PERFORMANCE later?+

Yes. If your business grows and you need more pages or enhanced features, upgrading is handled directly with Aitte after your initial term.
